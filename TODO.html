TORCS TO-DO List

Graphic:

  • Mirors
  • Fuel and dammage gauges on the dashboard
  • Camera and focus preselected when human player is racing
  • Non rotating camera type around the track

Simulation:

  • Tires (wear, temperature...)
  • Full 3D for the car displacements
  • Clutch (to start from rest)
  • Better ground surface definition
  • Weather (Temperature, rain, snow...)

Race Management:

  • New race engine more generic to let the race managers modules concentrate on the race rules
  • VCR
  • Network mode
  • Pace car
  • Start procedures
  • Intermediate time around the track
  • Limited time events and/or limited laps (qualifications, endurance races...)
  • Customisable races:
    • Qualif type
    • Number of rounds
    • Starting order for the rounds:
      • last round arrival order (including qualifs)
      • last round arrival order inverted
    • pits order:
      • Qualifs standings
      • Race numbers
      • last season results
    • Use of Pace-Car during race to group the cars, or for accidents
  • Drivers categories:
    • Rookie: no dammages, no specific rules, no pit stops, high adherence...
    • Amateur: limited dammages, pit stops, medium adherence...
    • Pro: full dammages, full rules, pit stops, low adherence...
  • Race Rules:
    • blue flag for the cars being 1 or more laps behind
    • yellow flag when accidents (forbids overtaking)
    • black flag for penalty (drive through or elimination)
    • speed limit in the pits
    • pace car (forbids overtaking) for big accidents, or in the middle of race to group all the cars

Tracks:

  • Non circuit type
  • Free borders
  • Track compiler and optimizer (work in progress)
